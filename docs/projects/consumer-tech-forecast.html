Forecasting Methodology

Revenue is modelled with Holt-Winters triple exponential smoothing (period = 12 months), which decomposes the signal into level, trend, and seasonality components. The 95% prediction interval widens with forecast horizon, reflecting accumulated uncertainty. In the full R Shiny app, forecast::ets() automatically selects the optimal error/trend/seasonal variant (additive vs multiplicative vs damped) via AIC.
